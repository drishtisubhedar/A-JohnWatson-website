Cases

A STUDY IN PINK
The body of Jennifer Wilson, who was dressed in pink. Holmes deduces she is from out of town and therefore had a suitcase. The police have not found a carry-on with the body.

THE BLIND BANKER
At the National Antiquities Museum, Chinese pottery expert Soo Lin Yao sees something frightening and disappears. A break-in with seemingly meaningless pair of symbols spray-painted onto a portrait of a banker.

THE GREAT GAME
If Sherlock Holmes does not solve the puzzles, hostages die.

THE HOUNDS OF BASKERVILLE
Henry Knight, who witnessed his father's death by a "gigantic hound" at Dartmoor 20 years ago, revisits the site only to find the hound back there again.
